Privacy Policy

We collect only what we need to operate the MVP: your phone number, profile details, and order/chat data. Location sharing is optional and only used for order tracking.

What we collect

  • Account: phone number, name, role, hub (optional).
  • Orders: origin/destination text, order items, quotes, delivery status.
  • Chat: messages within an order.
  • Location: GPS coordinates only when you choose to capture/share it.

How we use it

  • To create and fulfill orders and display tracking on active deliveries.
  • To send in-app notifications about orders and messages.

This policy will be updated as the product evolves (payments, KYC, etc.).
